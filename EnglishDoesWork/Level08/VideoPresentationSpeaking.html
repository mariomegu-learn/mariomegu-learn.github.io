A Fun Day at the Park

Presentation

A few years ago, I went to the park with my friend Sarah. We were walking around when I noticed a large group of people gathered near the fountain.

Sarah asked me, "Do you think something special is happening there?"
I looked over and said, "Maybe they are just having a picnic or something".

Getting Closer

We decided to get closer, and then Sarah told me, "I think they’re waiting for someone important."

I was curious, so I asked, "Who could it be?"
Sarah smiled and replied, "I don’t know, but let’s find out!"

The Announcement

As we walked nearer, a woman took the microphone and announced, "Welcome, everyone! Thank you for coming to this surprise event!"

I turned to Sarah and whispered, "I didn’t expect this!"
Sarah laughed and said, "Neither did I!"

The Surprise Concert

It turned out to be a surprise concert! After the performance, Sarah told me, "I’m so glad we stayed. This was so much fun!"

I agreed and said, "Yeah, it was a perfect surprise. I’m happy we decided to come to the park that day."
